What is the uniqueness of the flower that is very often known by many people??

raflesia's flowies

Rafflesia is very unique because this species is only a bud or flower blossom, no stems, leaves, and roots. Besides the bud or flower, Rafflesia is only equipped with a haustorium, a tissue that has a root-like function that sucks photosynthetic food from the host plant.

sunflowies

Sunflowers don't always grow tall. There are two types of sunflowers based on their height: tall and short. Some types of sunflowers grow only 60 cm tall, while tall ones can reach 3-5 meters, even more. Young sunflowers have a unique habit of following the movement of the sun.
